Cheese Crepioca

Description

Crepioca is a brazilian food that combines Tapioca (another common brazilian food) and Crepe (a type of pancake).
Combining the carbohidrates of tapica flour or tapioca startch (originated from the roots of cassava plants) and the
protein from eggs, crepiocas are a good option for someone who is on a healthy or fit diet.

Ingredients

  • 40 grams of tapioca startch / tapioca flour
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 20 grams of mozzarella cheese (or any cheese of your liking)
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the tapioca startch and eggs.
  2. Slice the cheese into small cubes and mix with the tapioca.
  3. Add salt and other seasonings of you liking (parsley is a great option for this recipe!).
  4. In a preheated skillet, pour the mix and cook in low heat until the top of the crepioca is no-longer liquid.
  5. Flip the crepioca and cook until golden brown.
  6. You are ready to serve! Additionally you can stuff the crepioca with cheese like a taco.
