Proteic Microwave Banana Cake

Description

A great option for fit, healty and gluten-free diets, this banana cake is easy and fast to cook, using only a few
ingredients and providing protein for your diet!

Ingredients

  • 1 large banana (or alternatively 2 small bananas)
  • 30 grams of oat flakes
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 serving of the protein powder of your choice
  • 2 table spoons of coconut milk

Steps

  1. In a cup or microwavable container mash the banana until smooth.
  2. Mix with the oat flakes, eggs, protein powder and coconut milk until smooth.
  3. Microwave the batter from 90s to 2 minutes.
  4. You are done! Eat while hot or reserve for consuming later.

Cooking tip: you can add one table spoon of unsweetened cocoa powder to make a chocolate cake!
